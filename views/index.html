Rebalance the AI Power Disproportion

Most AI development today is made by large companies that are immensely resourceful. JARVIS lowers the entry barrier for all machine learning developers, knowledge curators, app developers, and computation providers, bringing the value of AI to more people in more ways.

Decentralized AI Marketplace

Combining AI with blockchain technology, JARVIS creates a viable decentralized AI marketplace for different types of stakeholders.

GPU Miners
Rent out computation power to complete AI training, AI serving, and other jobs on JARVIS.
Knowledge Curators
Collect and maintain valuable data and/or knowledge to ensure their availability on JARVIS.
Machine Learning Developers
Develop computer programs that continue to refine their performances through data analytics to find insights.
App Developers
Adopt turn-key AI solutions on JARVIS to create applications that deliver values to end-users.

JAR: The AI Token

JARVIS is introducing JAR as the utility token that facilitates the value exchange processes, such as training AI models, providing blockchain consensus, renting GPU resources, and much more. JAR is imperative to JARVIS’ economic model and crucial help define how JARVIS stakeholders will create, distribute, and consume AI models and curated knowledges.
